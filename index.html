Pillars of Faith & Kings Ministers

Seventh Day Adventist Choirs

Biography

Pillars of Faith Ministers & Kings Ministers are gospel groups of preachers & singers guided by the doctrines of the SDA church in the proclamation of the message of God’s kingdom

PILLARS OF FAITH

The group is composed of twenty (25) singers. Three (3) sponsors and a number of associates distributed in most parts of the Kenya. They are based at Victory SDA church in Kisumu, Central Nyanza Field of Western Kenya Union.
